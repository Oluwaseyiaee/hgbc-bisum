Business and Investment Summit

Business and Investment Summit(BISUM) is a transformative conference hosted by Higher Ground Baptist Church. It is a platform designed to empower minds, ignite creativity, and equip participants with the tools to excel in business and investments. This summit brings together industry experts, successful entrepreneurs, and visionary leaders to share actionable insights, proven strategies, and innovative ideas that help people maximize their potential and navigate the ever-evolving financial landscape. BISUM has successfully produced numerous entrepreneurs and leaders who excel in their respective fields.

BISUM is a great opportunity for aspiring entrepreneurs, seasoned business owners, and individuals seeking to enhance their financial literacy. It features engaging workshops, interactive sessions, and networking opportunities, providing the knowledge and connections needed to make informed decisions and turn ideas into reality.

Wealth Magnet

This year’s Business and Investment Summit (BISUM) is your golden opportunity to transform your financial future under the powerful theme, Wealth Magnet.
